Hi-Lo

A guessing game

To play, the dealer will draw the first card from a deck. Your goal is to guess if the next card will be higher or lower than that card.

  • Guess correctly 3 times in a row and you can choose to swap roles with the dealer or keep guessing.
  • If you guess incorrectly, you get a point for each card in the discard pile, then those cards are cleared and your guesses start over at 0.
  • Winner is the person with the fewest points when the cards run out.
